How to connect the Bluetooth smartwatch to make or answer calls on the watch?

You need to download the APP from your phone’s APP store, turn on your bluetooth and open the APP, click “Add A Device” , then find “P42B” to pair it.

Android: After successfully connecting the APP, the mobile phone will automatically pop up “Pair”or”OK” in 5 seconds to request a Bluetooth call. It does not need to be set on the mobile phone separately. After the binding is successful, you can answer and make calls on the watch.

iPhone: After the APP is successfully bound, you need to open the Phone settings → Bluetooth on the phone, find Bluetooth named “P42B_Phone” in the list, and click “Pair”. After the binding is successful, you can answer and make calls on the watch.
