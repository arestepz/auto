Panasonic KX-TGD610B

Cordless Home Phone System with Easy Call Block

Make working from home easier and stress-free by stopping nuisance calls in 1 step. Expandable to 6 handsets, the KX-TGD610B is equipped with Smart Call Block features to avoid nuisance callers and scammers, plus many other features for day-to-day convenience.

See and Dial Clearly

Bright, large display on handset units makes it easy to see who's calling in the language you choose (English or Spanish display). And, a backlit keypad makes it easy to dial even in low-light situations.

Easily Block Unwanted Callers

Say good-bye to scam calls and telemarketers. A one-touch Call Block button lets you easily block nuisance callers. Store up to 150 unwanted caller numbers.

Expand Your System

Keep additional handsets virtually anywhere you need in your home, expanding your phone system up to 6 cordless handsets (sold separately).

9-Number Speed Dial

Store up to 9 numbers on Speed Dial for quick access to important contacts.

Noise Reduction Technology

Reduce the background noise on the caller’s end so you can hear the voice of the person you are talking to more clearly.

100-Number Phone Book

Make calls without having to dial manually with a 100-number memory phonebook.

Speakerphone Doubles as a Baby Monitor*

Place a cordless handset unit in baby's room to audibly monitor from different areas of the house or even while away from home! The monitored handset will call the monitoring handset, base unit, or a stored phone number when it detects sound. *Additional handset (KX-TGDA66M or KX-TGDA63W) required.

Long Battery Life with Audible Low Battery Alert

Enjoy up to 10 hours talk time and 5 days of standby on handsets (while in eco mode). And, you never have to worry about the handset running out of power - audible alerts and visual icons make sure you know when it's time to charge.
