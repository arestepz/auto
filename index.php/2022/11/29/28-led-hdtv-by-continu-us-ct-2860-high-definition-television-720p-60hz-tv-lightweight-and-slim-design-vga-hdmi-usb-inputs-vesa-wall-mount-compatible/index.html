VESA Wall Mount Pattern and TV Dimensions

This TV has a Universal VESA Pattern of 100×100 which is one of the most common patterns, meaning it will fit most TV Wall Mounts that are manufactured.

VESA is a universally known measurement of TV mounts used with most TV companies around the world.A VESA hole pattern is measured in both horizontal and vertical distance between mounting holes.

For example, VESA 100 x 100 means your TV mounting holes are 100mm apart horizontally and 100mm apart vertically.
