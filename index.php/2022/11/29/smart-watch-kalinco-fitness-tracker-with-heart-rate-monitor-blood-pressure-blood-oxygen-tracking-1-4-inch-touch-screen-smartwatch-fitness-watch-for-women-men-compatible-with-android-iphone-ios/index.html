How to monitor your blood pressure and blood oxygen?

1. Set personal information in the App.

2. Wear the watch 1.5-2.5cm below the wrist bone.

3. Enter the monitoring mode and keep your arm stable.

4. Wait 10 ~15 seconds to show the data.

Note: the data cannot be used as a medical-grade test.

How to reconnect the smart watch with smartphones?

For iPhone: Remove Device ‘P22’ in the HeroBandIII App→Open ‘Settings’→Click ‘8luetooth’→Select ‘P22’→Click ‘（i）’ →Click ‘Forget This Device’→Reconnect the smart watch in App

For Android: Remove ‘P22’ in the App→Reconnect the smart watch in App

How to get message notifications on the watch if you do not allow the App to receive notifications when installing App?

1. For iOS: ‘Phone Settings’→Click ‘8luetooth’→’P22’→Turn on ‘Share System Notification’

For Android: Open App→’Notifications’→’Notification access’→Turn on ‘HeroBandIII’

2.Open ‘Phone Settings’→’Notification’→’HeroBandⅢ’→Turn on ‘Allow Notification’

How to reset time on the watch?

The watch time is synced with the connected smartphone.

If the watch time is wrong, please unbind with phone first.

Then follow the steps above to reconnect with smartphone to get the correct time.

How to track your steps correctly?

1. Set personal information correctly in the APP.

2. Wear the watch 1.5-2.5 cm below the wrist bone, and adjust the strap not too tightly or too loosely.

3. Let your arms swing naturally while walking.
